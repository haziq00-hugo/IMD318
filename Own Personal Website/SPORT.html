Horseback Archery

Horseback archery is a sport that combines riding a horse and shooting arrows with a bow while in motion. The archer rides the horse and aims at targets, often while the horse is galloping. It requires skill in both archery and horsemanship, as the archer must control the horse while accurately shooting arrows. It's a traditional practice in some cultures and has become a competitive sport in recent years.
